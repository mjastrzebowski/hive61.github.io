hive61 is Poznań's Startup and Business Community. It's a series of meetups for people with entrepreneurial drive in Poznań.

Hive is a dynamic entrepreneurial community. We invite everyone involved in startups, businesses or any other creative organizations based in Poznań.

From time to time we will be organizing meetings, preceded by valuable presentations given by known speakers. Each will take place in different location - because it's a hive and it's not connected to one location.

You ask why "61"? That's simple - it Poznań's postal code :)

Presentation will be often held in English. We'd like anyone interested to be able to join in.
